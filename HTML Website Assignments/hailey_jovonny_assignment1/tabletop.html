Table Top Games Aren't That Bad

I recently got into table-top games. Most notably Dungeons & Dragons and Open Legends. These two are Role Playing Games that I never thought I would get into, but they are really fun. I started playing them with a group of friends on campus. They got me into Open Legends, which is like D&D but with a twist. I won't get into the details, but it's only slightly different with more identity towards character creation. This game my gateway into D&D which I play weekly with another group of friends on Discord.

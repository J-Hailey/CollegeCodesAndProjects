Walking keeps me healthy

Every once in a while, I like to go on long walks through my neighborhood. This is something that is healthy for me to do. Not only physically, but mentally as well. When I have rough day, or if I just need to clear my head. It allows me to get away from not just reality, but the virtual world as well. It's the healthiest way for me to get off the grid, even if it's just for 30 minutes.
